Why To Visit Uttarakhand

  • Uttarakhand is home to the Jim Corbett National Park, the first national park in India.
  • Experience the Char Dham Yatra, one of the most revered pilgrimage circuits in India.
  • The holy city of Haridwar is famous for the Ganga Aarti and is one of the seven holiest places in Hinduism.
  • Uttarakhand is home to the Valley of Flowers, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning floral diversity.
  • The Auli Ski Resort is one of the top skiing destinations in India, offering scenic mountain views.
  • The Nanda Devi Peak, the second-highest mountain in India, stands majestically in Uttarakhand's landscape.
  • Rishikesh, known as the Yoga Capital of the World, offers spiritual experiences and adventure sports.
