Why To Visit Gujarat

  • The only habitat of the Asiatic Lions in India Gir Forests.
  • The state is home to Statue of Unity, the world's tallest statue depicting Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel
  • Gandhinagar is considered Asia's greenest capital city
  • This state has the largest district in India, known as Kachchh.
  • The Rann of Kachchh is the largest salt desert in the world.
  • The Lakshmi Vilas Palace in Vadodara is the largest private residence built in India. It is four times bigger than the Buckingham Palace in London.
  • Located near Bhuj, Mandvi is a coastal destination that boasts a 400-year-old shipbuilding tradition.
